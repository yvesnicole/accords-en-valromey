Founded in 2024, the **Accords en Valromey** festival was born from the conviction that classical music deserves to be alive, accessible, and rooted in its community.

Each summer, in the church of Champagne-en-Valromey, the festival brings together talented young musicians for intimate and captivating concerts. The exceptional acoustics of this heritage venue offer a unique sonic experience, where every note resonates with power and sincerity.

Our ambition is simple: to create moments of sharing and emotion between artists and audience, in a natural and welcoming setting. The Valromey, with its rolling landscapes and architectural heritage, provides the perfect setting for this musical and human adventure.
